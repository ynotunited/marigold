01

The Mark

The shield-and-M monogram stands for protection of quality and craft. It always leads; the wordmark follows underneath, never beside it, separated by the signature hairline.

Primary lockup — full colourMin. width 140px
On warm white / paper surfacesDefault use
On ink surfaces — sparinglyFooter, packaging only

Clear space on all sides equals the height of the shield mark. Never let type, imagery, or UI chrome enter that margin.

Don't

Recolour the mark. Gold is not optional — no black, no brand-colour swaps.

Place on busy photography or low-contrast backgrounds without a paper card behind it.

Stretch, rotate, or separate the shield from the wordmark in primary contexts.

04

Voice

Marigold Signature speaks the way the store looks: unhurried, precise, never shouting about a sale. Confidence over exclamation points.

We are

Understated

"Restocked in three finishes." Not "HUGE RESTOCK — DON'T MISS OUT!!"

Specific

"Hand-finished brass hardware." Not "premium quality materials."

Warm, not familiar

"Your order is on its way." Not "heyyy your stuff's coming!"

We are not

FLASH SALE 70% OFF TODAY ONLY

Discount language reads as mass-market. Frame value as craft and scarcity, not markdown percentages.

😍🔥 obsessed with this drop

No emoji as functional UI or in brand copy. Tone stays composed even in promotions.

Buy now before it's gone!!!

Urgency is shown through real inventory counts, not exclamation marks.
