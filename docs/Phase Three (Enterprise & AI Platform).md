# Phase Three Overview

## Objective

Transform Marigold from a premium ecommerce platform into an intelligent Corporate Merchandise Operating System (CMOS).

The system should assist employees, customers and management with automation, decision-making and business intelligence.

At this stage, Marigold becomes an enterprise platform rather than simply an online store.
