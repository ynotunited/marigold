# 1. Project Overview

## Background

Marigold Signature Nigeria Limited is a premium promotional merchandise and corporate gifting company that supplies branded products for businesses, institutions, events and organizations.

The current website functions primarily as a product catalogue with ecommerce capabilities. While functional, it does not fully communicate Marigold's premium positioning, nor does it provide the operational tools needed to support long-term growth.

The objective of this project is to build a completely custom ecommerce platform that serves as both a customer-facing luxury shopping experience and an internal business management system.

The platform must initially remain lightweight enough for deployment on shared hosting while being architected for future expansion into a full enterprise platform.

Marigold Commerce Platform (Internal)

## Vision Statement

To build Africa's most refined corporate gifting ecommerce experience—one that combines premium design, effortless shopping, quotation workflows, and scalable business operations.

The platform should inspire confidence in first-time visitors while remaining intuitive for returning corporate buyers.

## Core Principles

The platform should always prioritize:

• Simplicity over complexity

• Premium over flashy

• Performance over unnecessary features

• Scalability over shortcuts

• Accessibility over exclusivity

• Storytelling over clutter

# 2. Product Vision

The platform is not intended to become another generic ecommerce website.

Instead, it should become the digital flagship of Marigold.

Visitors should feel they are interacting with a premium brand before they ever purchase a product.

The experience should communicate craftsmanship, trust, quality, and professionalism.

Products should not simply be displayed.

They should be presented as tools that elevate brands, strengthen business relationships and create memorable experiences.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Phase One establishes a premium ecommerce experience.

Phase Two transforms the website into an operational management platform.

Phase Three introduces automation, intelligent recommendations, enterprise workflows and AI-powered experiences.

Every phase builds upon the previous one without requiring major architectural changes.

# 4. Design Philosophy

The interface should feel modern without becoming trendy.

The design should emphasize confidence rather than decoration.

The user should immediately understand that Marigold specializes in premium corporate merchandise.

Whitespace should become part of the design language.

Motion should guide attention rather than entertain.

The interface should remain clean regardless of the number of products added in future.

---

## Design Inspiration

Rather than copying competitors, the platform combines proven patterns from world-class digital products.

### Apple

For storytelling, whitespace and premium presentation.

### Stripe

For modern layouts and B2B credibility.

### Bellroy

For product photography and lifestyle merchandising.

### Printful

For ecommerce workflows and customization.

### Aesop

For typography and refined minimalism.
